# Expense Tracker

A personal finance app for tracking daily income and expenses. Built with Ethiopian Birr (ETB) as the default currency.

---

## What it does

You log transactions, it keeps score. At the top of the home screen you see your current month's balance — income minus expenses. There's a monthly trend chart so you can see whether things are getting better or worse over time.

The app has five tabs: Home, Transactions, Analytics, Budget, and Settings. Most of your time is spent on the first two.

---

## Screens

**Home** — balance for the current month, quick income/expense totals, and a trend chart. Hit the `+ Add` button to log a transaction.

**Transactions** — a running list of everything you've recorded.

**Analytics** — breakdowns by category so you can see where the money is actually going.

**Budget** — set spending limits and track how close you are.

**Settings** — currency, theme, app lock, and CSV export.

---

## Settings

- **Theme** — light or dark
- **Currency** — defaults to Br (ETB), can be changed
- **App lock** — toggle on if you want the app gated behind your phone's lock
- **Export transactions** — dumps everything to a CSV file
- **Version** — 1.0.0

---

## Getting started

Open the app, tap `+ Add`, pick income or expense, fill in the amount and category. That's it. Do that consistently and the charts start to mean something after a few weeks.
